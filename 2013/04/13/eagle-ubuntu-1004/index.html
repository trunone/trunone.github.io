執行 eagle 所需函式庫 (Ubuntu 10.04)

$ wget ftp://ftp.cadsoft.de/eagle/program/6.0/eagle-lin-6.4.0.run
$ sed -e '1,/^__DATA__$/d' eagle-lin-6.0.0.run > eagle-lin-6.4.0.tar.bz2
$ tar xvjf eagle-lin-6.4.0.tar.bz2
$ cd eagle-6.4.0/bin
$ wget http://dl.dropbox.com/u/4295670/eagle_libs.tar.bz2
$ tar xvjf eagle_libs.tar.bz2
$ echo 'LD_LIBRARY_PATH="$(dirname "$0")" "$(dirname "$0")/eagle" $@' > eagle.run
$ chmod +x eagle.run
